Web Design

By listening to what your goals are I can design clean and effective websites with usability in mind. All of my websites are optimized for the user as well as search engines. By making a site stand up to standards I can ensure a quality product that can be viewed predictably in all major browsers.

CMS and Development

Getting your site online is easy, but making sure it is done cost effectively requires planning. Using a Content Management System allows the user to make and update content with little knowledge and almost no HTML experience.

By using a CMS you will cut costs in maintenance and allow your website to become “Future Proof” and allows more advanced features like SEO and search to be done more effectively.

Content Strategy

Everything is content. But I can take inventory of your existing content and make sure its used in the most efficient way. Part of making a website easy to use is making the content easy to find and meaningful, if you don’t meet one or both of those goals your user will simply move on.

User Research

Wouldn’t you like to see the future? Or at least the outcome of your project before you commit your budget to any one thing?

By creating a prototype you remove all the smoke and mirrors and are left with an idea that will either need improvement or will reinforce your direction. You can make informed decisions based on your target audiences success or failure.

Search Engine Optimization or “SEO”

Not all web sites are created equal, but they all share the same space. SEO helps improve traffic by using meaningful Keywords and Meta data. Your sites content and markup can both be optimized to promote your websites placement in search results.

Social Media Optimization or “SMO”

Get people connected to your product, generate publicity through social media and online communities with RSS feeds, social news buttons, and blogging. SMO’s primarily the focus on driving traffic from sources other than search engines, though improved search ranking is also a benefit of successful SMO.
